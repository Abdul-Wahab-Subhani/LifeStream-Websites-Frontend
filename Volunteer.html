Join Us and Make a Difference!

Camp Information

Join us in supporting our community! As a volunteer, you’ll help with different aspects of the camp, from organizing to providing essential support. Your involvement will make a significant difference.

Why Volunteer?

  • Make a positive impact in your community.
  • Learn new skills and gain valuable experience.
  • Meet like-minded individuals and build connections.

Volunteer Roles:

  • Organizer: Manage the camp’s logistics and ensure everything runs smoothly.
  • Helper: Assist with daily tasks, setup, and support camp participants.
  • Medical Support: Provide first aid and help those in need of medical attention.
  • Logistics: Coordinate transportation, food, and essential supplies.
