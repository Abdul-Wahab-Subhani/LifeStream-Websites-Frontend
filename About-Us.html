Every Blood Donor Is A Lifesaver

Purpose

The purpose of LifeStream is to increase awareness and simplify the donation process,ensuring a reliable and timely supply of blood. This platform connects donors with recipients efficiently, promoting voluntary blood donation across the country.

Mission

To save lives by providing safe, reliable, and accessible blood donation services. We aim to create a compassionate community of donors dedicated to ensuring a steady and sufficient blood supply for those in need.

Vision

A world where every person in need of blood receives it promptly, and voluntary blood donation becomes a universal and regular practice, fostering a healthier and more connected society.

History

We at LifeStream Lahore live by the motto of saving lives by ensuring every drop of blood contributes towards the right cause of saving lives. A mission started by young students from Lahore, LifeStream commenced operations on 22nd June 2024 as a project under the National Service Scheme (NSS) chapter. Soon, the young team lived by the fact that blood can't be manufactured in factories and committed themselves towards connecting generous donors to the donees. They worked around the clock towards this cause, and in no time, LifeStream Lahore became a full-scale NGO with a mission of solving the problem of blood shortage in Pakistan. The organization now caters to any requirement pertaining to blood and unites people in need of blood with voluntary blood donors.

Our Activities

Since its inception, LifeStream has developed a 360-degree solution to cater to the problem of blood shortage in the country. The model revolves around four focal points:

  • Ensuring continuous, sufficient blood supply to blood banks through blood donation camps and weekly donations
  • Increasing awareness by organizing Street Plays, Info-Talks, Competitions
  • Helping those in dire need by operating an emergency 24x7 helpline
  • Establishing a network of youth across the country to lead the movement
