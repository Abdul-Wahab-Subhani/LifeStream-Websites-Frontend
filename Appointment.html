Save Lives with Your Blood Donation

Why Donate Blood?

Every donation can save up to three lives. Your generous act can make a significant difference to those in need. Join our community of heroes and make a difference today by giving the gift of life. Your donation matters more than you can imagine.

Benefits of Donating Blood

  • Blood is needed for surgeries, cancer treatments, chronic illnesses, and traumatic injuries.
  • Donating blood can reduce harmful iron stores and may reduce the risk of heart disease.
  • Your donation supports local hospitals and patients in your community.

Who Can Donate?

Most people are eligible to donate blood if they:
  • Are in good health
  • Are at least 16 years old (varies by state)
  • Weigh at least 50kg (varies by donation type)
  • Have not donated blood in the last 56 days

Use this tool to quickly determine if you are eligible to donate blood. Answer a few simple questions to find out.
